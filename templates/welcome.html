AiBot chats and can answer some of your questions

AiBot is an AIML chatbot which is an offshoot of Dr. Richard Wallace's ALICE artificial intelligence program. AiBot is designed to be more "human" than ALICE. His database contains no information about being a chatbot. AiBot "thinks" he is a 20 year old college student living in India. For best results when talking to AiBot, use correct spelling and grammar, enter only one sentence at a time, and don't use a lot of internet slang.
